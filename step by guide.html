Step by Step Guide

  1. Complete high school with a focus on art or design subjects.
  2. Enroll in a diploma or degree program in Fashion Design, Industrial Design, or 3D Modeling.
  3. Take specialized courses in CAD (Computer-Aided Design) and 3D software like Blender or CLO3D.
  4. Build a strong portfolio of physical or digital model designs.
  5. Intern with fashion houses, design studios, or 3D animation companies.
  6. Participate in design competitions and exhibitions to gain exposure.
  7. Stay updated with trends, attend workshops, and continuously learn new tools.
  8. Apply for full-time roles as a junior model designer or freelance to gain real-world experience.
  9. Network with professionals and promote your work through an online portfolio or social media.
  10. Aim to specialize in your area of interest and grow toward leading your own creative projects.
